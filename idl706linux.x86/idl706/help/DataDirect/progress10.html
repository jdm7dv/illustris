The Progress OpenEdge Driver

The DataDirect Connect for ODBC Progress OpenEdge driver (the Progress OpenEdge driver) supports:

The Progress OpenEdge driver is supported in the Windows and UNIX environments. See "Environment-Specific Information" for detailed information about the environments supported by this driver.
